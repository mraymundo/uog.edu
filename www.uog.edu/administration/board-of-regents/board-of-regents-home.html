UOG Board of Regents

The University of Guam Board of Regents has general supervision over the University. The Board sets the policies governing the duties, conditions of employment, compensation, and salary of all University employees. The Board also submits  an annual budget to the Legislature. The Board of Regents meets no less than four (4) times a year.  Please contact Christine Mabayag at 735-2990 or Louise Toves at 735-2995 regarding scheduled meeting dates.
